Game Of PIG!
  1. Try out your LUCK!
  2. Dont Get Snake EYES!! Just Dont
  3. Read the instructions on the top after starting the game
  4. The first player to reach a score of 30 wins!

There are two players. The player whose turn it is rolls the dice. The total of the roll is added to the current player's score, unless either die comes up as a "one". I this happens, this player's turn is over, and it is the other playerâ€™s turn. After each roll, the current player can either roll again, (assuming a "one" was not rolled) or if the current player feels that luck is running thin, they can pass to the other player. The first player to get 30 points or higher wins.
Remember: if you roll two "ones" (snake eyes), your current score gets zeroed out.
